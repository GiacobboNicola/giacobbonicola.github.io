Hello,

my name is Nicola and I'm glad to welcome you on my personal page. Here you can find more information about my research, about me and, if you are interested, how to contact me.

Corrently, I'm a Ph.D. student at the University of Padova where I work with Michela Mapelli and her group. My research focuses on the study of the demography of compact-object binaries in different environments by means of population-synthesis simulations and N-body simulations. In particular, my primary goal is to better understand the evolutionary pathways of compact binaries and how they depend on the proprieties of the stellar progenitor (e.g. metallicity) and on the environment (e.g. star cluster versus field).
